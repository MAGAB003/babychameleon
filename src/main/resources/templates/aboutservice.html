The problem

Children grows very quickly, and parents get to buy a lot of new clothes often even before the clothes are worn out.

The solution

We offer subscription service for childrens clothes. We believe that sustainability, cost consciousness and circularity is important for our wallet as well as our planet. We want to offer our customers a new sustainable shopping experience that not only minimize the environmental impact but at the same time creates more time to experience the important things in life.
You recieve a package based on your choices. Either to home or to a pick-up point. When it's time for a size change you will recieve a new package and return the old clothes to be washed and reused. If you've chosen the laundry service you will recieve and send packages when you book a pickup for washing.
Step 1

You browse the service and look at packages/subscription options.


Step 2:

You choose which subscription package(s) you want


Step 3:

You create your your account


Step 4:

You choose which child/children the chosen subscription package(s) should be applied for or add a new child


Step 5:

Continue to checkout/shopping cart view, Choose payment method, Choose delivery method, start subscription
