About

Children grow very quickly, and parents needs to buy a lot of new clothes often even before the clothes are worn out.

We offer a subscription service for children's clothes. Sustainability, cost consciousness and circularity is important for us and our customers. We want to offer our customers a new sustainable shopping experience.
